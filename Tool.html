--- Alternate trigger interactions Press-and-hold: pop forward only while mouse is held. Good for “peek” behavior. Long-hover delay: 250 ms entrance, 150 ms exit. Reads as “intentional,” not jumpy. Sticky pin: click to pin the card in place; click outside to dismiss.
