Fascism — Full Definition

A far-right, authoritarian ultranationalist political system characterized by dictatorial power, suppression of dissent, regimentation of society and economy, and glorification of the state centered on a leader.

Core features

  • Authoritarian leadership
  • Ultranationalism
  • Militarism
  • Suppression of opposition
  • Scapegoating
  • Corporatist alignment
  • Propaganda & enemy construction
  • Anti-democratic practice
  • Cult of tradition
  • Emotional manipulation
